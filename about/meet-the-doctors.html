Meet Dr. Chad O Edwards, DDS

Dr. Edwards has extensive training in laser dentistry and restorative techniques, allowing him to perform many procedures with greater comfort and improved healing. His expertise also includes implant dentistry, full-mouth rehabilitation, cosmetic services, and advanced restorative care.

He is deeply committed to continued education, both as a lifelong learner and as an educator. Dr. Edwards regularly participates in professional training programs and teaches other dentists across the country in the use of laser technology — helping elevate the standard of care far beyond his own practice.

Dr. Ryan R. Torti, DDS

Dr. Ryan Torti brings a thoughtful, patient-centered approach to dentistry, blending advanced clinical training with a deep commitment to comfort and communication. Known for his calm, reassuring presence and meticulous attention to detail, Dr. Torti helps patients feel confident and cared for at every step of their treatment.

His areas of expertise include cosmetic dentistry, restorative treatments, Invisalign, and advanced laser dentistry. Dr. Torti's gentle approach — paired with his proficiency in modern technology — allows many patients to receive treatment with minimal or no anesthesia, reduced discomfort, and faster healing.

Dr. Torti is passionate about delivering results that look beautiful, function naturally, and support long-term oral health. He believes every patient deserves clarity in their dental care and enjoys educating patients so they feel empowered in their health decisions.

Outside the office: Dr. Torti enjoys spending time with family, teaching, traveling, and staying connected within the Franklin community.

Dr. Andy Burton, DDS

Dr. Andy Burton brings more than 30 years of experience in implant dentistry to Tennessee Center for Laser Dentistry, offering patients exceptional expertise in surgical placement, restorative design, and long-term implant success. His depth of clinical knowledge and commitment to precision allow him to deliver results that are both functional and beautifully natural.

A respected clinician in the Nashville region, Dr. Burton is known for his calm demeanor, meticulous approach, and dedication to providing each patient with clear communication and a comfortable experience. His broad skillset spans general, cosmetic, restorative, and implant dentistry, strengthened by ongoing training in emerging technologies and advanced techniques.

Dr. Burton is an active member of multiple professional organizations, including the Nashville Dental Society, the Tennessee Dental Association, the American Dental Association, the Tennessee Academy of Cosmetic Dentistry, the Academy of General Dentistry, and the Academy of Clear Aligners. He is also a Fellow in the World Clinical Laser Institute, reflecting his advanced proficiency in modern laser technology — an essential part of TNCLD's commitment to better science and better service.

Outside the office: Dr. Burton enjoys spending time with family, staying active in the dental community, and pursuing continued education that supports his passion for excellence in patient care.
