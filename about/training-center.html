Where Dentists Come to Learn

Training Center

At Tennessee Center for Laser Dentistry, our commitment to excellence extends beyond patient care. Adjacent to our practice is an on-site **Training Center** where dentists from across the country come to learn advanced techniques in technology-driven and laser-assisted dentistry.

Leaders in Modern Dentistry

Our doctors are trusted educators in the dental community, regularly training other professionals on the same technology and techniques we use every day in our practice. Teaching others reinforces our own standards — and reflects the level of expertise and precision we bring to patient care.

When dentists choose to learn from us, it’s because we don’t just talk about modern dentistry — we practice it.

A Purpose-Built Educational Space

Our Training Center is a dedicated, professional environment designed for hands-on learning, lectures, and small-group instruction. The space supports continuing education courses, technology training, and professional collaboration.

Facility Rental for Educational Events

In addition to our own training programs, the Training Center is available to rent for approved educational courses and professional events. If you’re interested in hosting a class, workshop, or training session, we’d be happy to share more details.

Why This Matters for Our Patients

Training other dentists keeps us at the forefront of modern dentistry. It means continuous learning, refined skills, and a standard of care that’s shaped by teaching, not just practicing.
